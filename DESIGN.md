# Design Reference

## Goals
- Fast, readable, and simple.
- Clean hierarchy and strong contrast.
- Sections feel modular and easy to update.

## Layout
- Full-width sections with a 12-column grid on desktop.
- Section anchors aligned to navbar height.
- Cards use consistent padding and spacing.

## Typography
- Base font: system sans-serif.
- Headings: light weight, large scale.
- Body text: 16-18px with relaxed line-height.

## Color Tokens
- --stimulus-yellow: primary accent.
- --stimulus-orange: section background.
- --stimulus-dark: dark panels.
- --stimulus-ink: strong text.
- --stimulus-text: body text.

## Components
- Navbar: transparent at top, white on scroll.
- Service cards: hover reveals technology badges.
- Buttons: pill shape, bold labels.
- Progress bars: simple and readable.

## Motion
- Transitions: 300-400ms ease for hover and scroll.
- Avoid heavy animations.

## Imagery
- Use full-bleed background images for hero and side panels.
- Keep image overlays subtle.

## Content Guidelines
- Keep copy short and direct.
- Use 3-5 bullet points for skill lists.
- Titles should be 2-4 words.
